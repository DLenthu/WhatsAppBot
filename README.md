# WhatsAppBot

A WhatsApp bot that learns and adapts your conversation styles based on who you're talking to.

## Idea

Different people get different versions of you:
- **Person A**: Formal, professional tone
- **Person B**: Casual, relaxed vibes
- **Person C**: English mixed with Telugu (Hinglish), inside jokes

Instead of manually adjusting your tone each time, WhatsAppBot learns your communication patterns and can generate responses that match your natural style with each person.

## How It Works

1. **Learn from history** — Analyze past conversations to understand your style with different people
2. **Adapt in real-time** — Continuously improve as the bot learns from corrections and patterns
3. **Respond as you** — Generate messages that sound authentically like you, adapted for the specific person
